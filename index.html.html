Notice of Data Privacy & Integrity

Sindarity Global is a Social Enterprise dedicated to secure advocacy infrastructure. The "U&I Have a Voice" tool and its associated AI workflows are designed for administrative organization and document synthesis only. This system does not provide legal, medical, or financial advice. All client data is processed within a secure, private environment to ensure HIPAA-aligned standards of confidentiality and trauma-informed care.

Your responses are stored only in your browser session and are never transmitted to a server without your explicit consent. The summary generated at the end is yours to save, print, or share with your own care team.
