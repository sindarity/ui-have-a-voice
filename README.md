## What This Is

The **U&I Have A Voice Tool** is a free, browser-based advocacy navigation tool built for families who are dismissed, misdiagnosed, or left without answers by the systems that were supposed to help them.

It takes a person's scattered medical records, verbal history, and lived experience — and synthesizes it into a **one-page Strategic Overview Summary (SOS Signal)** that any doctor, caseworker, or attorney can read and act on in under three minutes.

No login. No server. No data stored anywhere. Everything runs in your browser.

---

## Who It Is For

- People living with **invisible illness**, chronic conditions, or neurodiverse diagnoses
- **Kinship caregivers** navigating foster care, CPS, or emergency custody
- Families trying to navigate **SSDI, OOD, OhioKAN, IEP, or Medicaid** systems
- **Patient advocates, life coaches, and peer support professionals** who serve these populations
- Anyone who has ever arrived at an appointment and not known where to start

---

## What It Does

The tool walks through **6 guided steps**:

| Step | Section | What Happens |
|------|---------|--------------|
| 1 | Consent | Privacy notice confirmation — your data never leaves your browser |
| 2 | Your Story | Voice-to-text or typed intake of your current situation |
| 3 | Health & History | Diagnoses, dismissal patterns, document difficulty rating |
| 4 | What Has Been Tried | Services attempted, denials received, upcoming deadlines |
| 5 | Your Safest Path | Needs, goals, strengths, and priority outcome |
| 6 | SOS Signal | Auto-generated one-page summary with recommended next steps |

At the end, you can **download your SOS Signal as a PDF** — named, dated, and ready to hand to any professional.

---

## Key Features

- **Voice-to-text intake** — speak your story instead of typing it (Chrome/Edge)
- **Urgency scale** — color-coded 1–5 rating that appears on your summary
- **Pattern recognition** — the tool identifies gaps based on your responses
- **Safest Path Home** — a prioritized, sequenced action plan tailored to your situation
- **PDF download** — formatted, professional, ready to share
- **Zero data transmission** — nothing is sent anywhere; all processing is local
- **HIPAA-aligned design** — document synthesis only; no medical, legal, or financial advice

## Privacy & Compliance

> Sindarity Global is a Social Enterprise dedicated to secure advocacy infrastructure. ## How to Use

### Option 1 — Use the live tool (no setup needed)
Visit: **[sindarity.github.io/ui-have-a-voice](https://sindarity.github.io/ui-have-a-voice)**

Works on any device. No account required.

### Option 2 — Run it locally
```bash
# Clone this repository
git clone https://github.com/sindarityglobal/ui-have-a-voice.git

# Open the file in your browser
open index.html
```

That's it. No dependencies. No npm install. No build step. One HTML file.

### Option 3 — Host your own copy
Upload `index.html` to any static hosting service:
- **GitHub Pages** (free) — this repository uses it
- **Netlify** (free) — drag and drop the file at netlify.com
- **Google Sites** (free) — embed via HTML widget
